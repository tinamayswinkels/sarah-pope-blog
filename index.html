WORDS FOR SALE

Hi, I'm Sarah Pope. I'm a copywriter.

I sell words. Lots of words. But not these words.

These words are to tell you about myself and the wonderful world of copywriting so that you, in turn, may buy some of my words to sell your product and tell your story.

So, copywriting.

It's a funny word. And it covers a bunch of different stuff - from Blogging and Public Relations to Content Creation, Editing and Social Media Management.

But that's all a mouthful. Hence, copywriting.
