WORDS FOR SALE

Hi, I'm Sarah Pope. I'm a copywriter. 

Apparently, I'm also an 'oversharer.'

Which can be an awkward thing at dinner parties, job interviews and conversations with the in-laws. But in a digital world jam-packed full of advertising that says very little, it can be a good thing to over share. It's what makes you memorable and let's people get to know who you really are.

____________________

So, copywriting.

It's a word that lumps together a bunch of different stuff I do - from Blogging and Public Relations to Content Creation, Editing and Social Media Management. 

But that's a mouthful. Hence, copywriting.
